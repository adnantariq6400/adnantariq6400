Exploring the Impact of Type 2 Diabetes and Glucose-Lowering Drugs on Gut Microbiome Dynamics

Tariq, H.M.A., Khan, N.Y., Manzoor, H., & Kayani, M.U.R.
Springer Nature Discover Medicine Impact Factor: 5.2
Published: February 2025 DOI: 10.1007/s44337-025-00241-9 Microbiome, Diabetes, Metagenomics

This study investigates the complex relationship between type 2 diabetes, glucose-lowering medications, and gut microbiome composition, identifying significant microbial shifts associated with disease progression and treatment.

Prioritizing gut microbial SNPs linked to immunotherapy outcomes in NSCLC patients by integrative bioinformatics analysis

Raziq, M.F., Tariq, H.M.A., Khan, N., Manzoor, H., Rafiq, M., Rasool, S., Kayani, M.U.R., & Huang, L.
Journal of Translational Medicine Impact Factor: 6.7
Published: March 2025 DOI: 10.1186/s12967-025-06370-0 Immunotherapy, Microbiome, Bioinformatics

This research employs advanced bioinformatics approaches to identify gut microbial genetic variants associated with immunotherapy response in non-small cell lung cancer patients, providing potential biomarkers for treatment prediction.
