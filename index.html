About Me

I am a passionate Bioinformatician and NGS Data Analyst with expertise in metagenomics, machine learning, and computational biology. My research focuses on unraveling the complex interactions between hosts and their microbiomes, understanding antimicrobial resistance patterns, and developing innovative computational pipelines for large-scale genomic data analysis.

With a strong foundation in both biological sciences and computational methods, I bridge the gap between raw sequencing data and meaningful biological insights. My work contributes to advancing personalized medicine and our understanding of microbial ecosystems.
